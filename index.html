How about Arabica Casablanca ?Best coffee shop in the world

Our first store in Morocco, % Arabica Casablanca is located in Boulevard Anfa, Quartier Racine, in the heart of the city of Casablanca. Racine/Maarif is where 80% of the luxury brands, hotels, schools, and companies in the city are located. It is also home to one of the biggest stadiums in the country, Mohammed V Stadium, and the twin towers; the highest buildings in Morocco and North Africa.

Being just five minutes by car from the Mosque Hassan II, ten minutes away from the corniche, and five minutes away from downtown Casablanca, the store has an excellent location in one of the city’s main thoroughfares. Whether you are a local or a tourist, Racine welcomes you with open arms and provides you with anything you may be looking for, from work places, residential areas, restaurants, shopping centres, markets and of course, delicious coffee
